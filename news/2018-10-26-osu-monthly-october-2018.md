This month's issue of osu!monthly features behind-the-scenes interviews, a recap of MWC, a look into the mapping mentorship program, stat-rounding tutorials and more. Come check it out!

[![](/wiki/shared/news/2018-10-26-osu-monthly-october-2018/banner.png)](https://osumonthly.com/2018-10)

It's been a while, but we're finally returning! This October, I got most of the team back together to produce another edition of the osu!monthly magazine. This one features:

- An osu! news recap
- In-depth commentary on the osu!mania 4K World Cup
- Behind-the-scenes insight with Ephemeral
- Guides for achieving the perfect osu! stats
- "Event Hub", a summary of community events
- A personal experience with the Mapping Mentorship program
- "Scorewatch", a look back at the most prominent scores
- An interview with Kurokami about the state of osu! Spotlights

Unlike the other issues of osu!monthly, this is in a web format instead of a PDF, so hopefully it'll be easier for everybody to access. Please view it on a desktop or laptop computer for the best experience!

[Check out the full magazine on our website!](https://osumonthly.com/2018-10) We hope you enjoy it!
